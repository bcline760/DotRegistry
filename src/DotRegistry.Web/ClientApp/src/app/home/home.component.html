Providers

What are Terraform providers?

Terraform providers are essentially plugins that implement data and resource types. Typical examples of providers are AzureRM and AWS that are found on Hashicorp's public repository.

Modules

What are Terraform modules?

Terraform modules are reusable snippets of Terraform configuration. They are groups of resources combined into one and are managed as if a single resource.
